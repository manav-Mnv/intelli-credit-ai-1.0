The Problem with Corporate Loan Analysis

Banks currently analyze loans manually. Analysts read 100+ page financial reports, risk signals are hidden in unstructured documents, and the approval process takes days or weeks.

Slow Approvals

Approval process takes days or weeks manually analyzing lengthy, complex reports.

Human Bias

Subjective analysis leading to inconsistent and risky lending decisions.

Missed Risk Signals

Critical risk signals hidden deep in unstructured documents are often overlooked.

High Operational Cost

Excessive manual effort required per loan application, driving up processing costs.

The IntelliCredit AI Solution

Automated, data-driven credit risk analysis. Loan decisions in minutes, not weeks.

1. Upload Documents

System ingests PDFs, bank statements, and legal notices.

2. AI Extracts Financial Data

OCR and NLP parse tables and retrieve unstructured risk signals.

3. Risk Models Analyze Company

4-layer engine: Rule-based ratios, XGBoost ML, NLP keyword signals, and sector risk weighting.

4. Research Agents Gather Intelligence

AI automatically fetches external news, litigation history, and industry trends.

5. AI Generates Credit Appraisal Memo

⚡ Loan decisions in minutes

Hackathon Challenge: The 3 Pillars

IntelliCredit AI comprehensively addresses all core requirements.

1. Data Ingestor

Handles multiple formats including PDF Annual Reports, GST Returns, Bank Statements, Legal Notices, and Loan Sanctions.

Tools Used:

PyMuPDF (PDFs) Tesseract (OCR) Camelot (Tables) Pandas (Excel)
Example Detection:

Revenue inflation (GST revenue vs bank deposits), Circular trading.

2. Research Agent

AI automatically gathers external intelligence to triangulate risk profiles.

  • News Agent: Fraud news, investigations, media signals
  • Litigation Agent: Lawsuits, NCLT, DRT tribunals
  • Industry Agent: Sector growth trends & risks
  • Graph Agent: Neo4j director & entity link analysis

3. Recommendation Engine

Final AI decision system combining financial ratios, ML scores, NLP, external research, and graph analysis.

Output Example →
Risk Score: 38.5 / 100
Risk Grade: Medium
Default Probability: 28.4%
Interest Rate: 11.5%
✅ Approve — ₹10 Cr · 60 months
