System Deep Dives

Comprehensive technical specifications of IntelliCredit AI's core engines.

Document Processing Engine

Transforms unstructured pixels and text into structured datasets.

Tooling Arsenal

  • PyMuPDF: Lightning-fast text extraction from digitally native PDFs.
  • Camelot: Targets lattice and stream-based tables (e.g., balance sheets).
  • pdfplumber: Fallback for complex visual bounding-box extractions.
  • Tesseract OCR: Utilized for scanned images or non-searchable PDFs.

Financial Intelligence Engine

Structures raw data into rigorous accounting metrics and underwriting ratios.

Metrics & Ratios (10+ Computed)

  • Profitability: Profit Margin, Gross Margin, EBITDA Margin, ROE, ROCE.
  • Leverage: Debt-to-Equity (D/E), Debt-to-Assets, TOL/TNW ratio.
  • Liquidity: Current Ratio flagged below 1.0 (critical) and 1.2 (tight).
  • Debt Service: DSCR and Interest Coverage Ratio — most critical for lenders.
  • Growth: YoY Revenue Growth vs previous year comparison.
  • Health Score: Composite 0–100 score used to seed the risk engine.

Machine Learning Risk Engine

Quantitative XGBoost model trained on historical corporate default data.

  • The Model: XGBClassifier optimized for tabular financial data.
  • Feature Engineering: Uses computed financial ratios, company age, and sector risks.
  • Outputs: Default probability percentage and human-readable risk score.
  • Explainability: Employs SHAP (SHapley Additive exPlanations) for regulatory compliance.

NLP Risk Analysis (FinBERT)

Scans unstructured text for qualitative risks that pure numbers miss.

  • Sentiment Analysis: Evaluates Director's Reports and Management Discussions.
  • Risk Phrase Detection: Isolates sentences indicating "going concern" issues or auditor qualifications.
  • Integration: Highly negative textual sentiment acts as a multiplier, pulling down the overall XGBoost credit score.

Autonomous Intelligence Agents

LangChain agents reach outside provided documents to eliminate information asymmetry.

  • News Agent (news_agent.py): Scrapes financial news for fraud, C-suite changes, or tax investigations.
  • Litigation Agent (litigation_agent.py): Queries legal databases for NCLT, DRT, and ongoing lawsuits.
  • Industry Agent (industry_agent.py): Pulls sector-wide trends to verify projected growth realism.
  • Graph Agent (graph_agent.py): Queries Neo4j to detect shell-company links, bad directors, and entity overlap.

Vector Knowledge Base

RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) system for interacting with 100+ page documents.

  • Text Chunking: LangChain's RecursiveCharacterTextSplitter preserves context.
  • Embedding Creation: High-dimensional vectors via OpenAI/Sentence-Transformers.
  • ChromaDB Retrieval: Semantic nearest-neighbor retrieval surfaces exact clauses for analyst Q&A.

Graph Risk Engine

Models systemic and interconnected credit risks using Neo4j.

  • Nodes & Relationships: Companies, Directors, Addresses with links like OWNS_SHARES_IN.
  • Hidden Risk Detection: Graph algorithms detect "shell-company matrices" and hidden promoter risks (e.g., a director involved in a separate bankrupt entity).

AI Credit Reasoning & CAM Generation

The cognitive synthesis layer aligning all vectors with the traditional Five Cs of Credit.

The Five Cs Synthesis

  • Character: Promoter background checks & Graph history.
  • Capacity: XGBoost risk score & cash flows.
  • Capital: Debt-to-Equity & Net Worth metrics.
  • Collateral: Asset registry matrix parsing.
  • Conditions: Industry Agent's macro-economic assessment.

Generated CAM Report

  • Format: Standardized Markdown/PDF report with an Executive Summary.
  • Transparent Justification: The LLM explicitly drafts a justification recommending Approval, Rejection, or Manual Review, directly citing the underlying data points.
